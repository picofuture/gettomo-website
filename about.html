What is Tomo?

Tomo started from real conversations with vibe coders, builders hitting roadblocks when AI-generated code failed silently.

I kept seeing the same thing: the AI didn't know enough about the system to debug properly, and its fixes just made things worse.

Enter Tomo, an AI that is meant to be your personal friend on your vibe-coding journey. It injects itself into your codebase from day 1 and builds up a deep understanding of your system. This allows it to do a better job at answering your questions and helping you fix issues than the AI you are currently using.

Tomo's mission is to help you build scalable apps just like a professional software engineer and stay in control of your codebase.
