Frequently Asked Questions

When will I be able to access Tomo?

Tomo is currently in active development. I'm working closely with a small group of beta testers to push its accuracy beyond 80%. If you're on the waitlist, you'll get weekly progress updates and early access as soon as it's ready.

Will Tomo train on my codebase?

No. Tomo doesn't learn from your code directly. I extract conceptual insights manually and apply them to improve Tomo's reasoning. Your code remains private and untouched by any training loop.
